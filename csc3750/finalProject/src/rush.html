Rush

Interested in rushing Theta Tau?

Theta Tau offers a unique opportunity to undergraduate engineering students. The fraternity offers a multitude of resources and provides an exciting and supportive environment. Some benefits of joining Epsilon Beta chapter include:

Academic Resources
The brothers of Epsilon Beta chapter are taking the same classes you are. Our fraternity house library contains electrical, computer, mechanical, civil, industrial, and chemical engineering as well engineering tech, and computer science textbooks. Members also keep their class notes and are available for tutoring. Fraternity members of similar standings often take classes together and study in groups.

The process

During rush there is no obligation, fee, or any concrete requirement from us.

Over the course of the first month of the semester we the Fraternity will host a large number of events where we invite everyone to get to know us.

As the month winds down we hand out bids to formally invite people that we believe will be a good fit for the chapter to attend a formal event.

Criteria for bidding

In order to receive a bid you must fit the following criteria:

  • Be an undergraduate student in good academic standing
  • Have a College of Engineering approved academic major
  • Not be in the first semester of your academic program
  • Not be in the graduating semester of your academic program
